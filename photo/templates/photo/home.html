Creative Portraiture

Senior Portraits

Senior Portraits celebrate the uniqueness and achievements of high school seniors as they embark on an exciting new chapter in their lives. My goal is to create stunning and timeless portraits that capture the style, personality, and accomplishments of every senior, providing them and their families with cherished memories to last a lifetime.

During your photoshoot, I will create a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere, making the senior feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera. I encourage them to express themselves, to laugh, to be spontaneous, and to embrace their individuality. By fostering a positive and supportive environment, we will be able to capture genuine smiles, laughter, and emotions that radiate through their portraits.

Airborne Images

Drone Photography

As a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) licensed and insured drone pilot, I am dedicated to capturing awe-inspiring images and creating breathtaking videos from above. Specializing in aerial photography, my passion lies in harnessing the power of drone technology to provide you with unique perspectives that transcend traditional photography.

My commitment to professionalism extends to every aspect of my work. I prioritize safety above all else and strictly adhere to FAA regulations and guidelines. Additionally, I carry comprehensive liability insurance to provide you with peace of mind, knowing that your project is in capable hands.

Take a moment to explore my portfolio and witness the magic of aerial photography. I invite you to embark on this thrilling visual journey with me, where imagination truly takes flight. Contact me today to discuss your project and let's create something extraordinary together.
