About Me!

Hello, my name is Tom Stout, and I am an experienced photographer with a passion for capturing meaningful moments. With over 30 years of professional experience, I have honed my skills and developed a keen eye for detail. Throughout my career, I have specialized in various areas of photography, including portraits, weddings, and drone photography and videography. Each genre presents unique challenges and opportunities for creativity, and I thrive on capturing the essence of my subjects and their stories.

In portrait photography, I strive to create images that reflect the true personality and emotions of the individuals I photograph. Whether it's a heartfelt smile, a contemplative gaze, or a moment of pure joy, I believe that the best portraits are those that evoke genuine emotions and create lasting memories. Weddings are another area of photography that I hold dear to my heart. I consider it an honor to be part of such a special day and strive to capture the love, excitement, and beauty of each couple's journey. From intimate moments shared between the bride and groom to the grandeur of the ceremony and celebration, I aim to document the day in a way that tells a unique and captivating story.

Additionally, I have embraced the advancements in technology and have incorporated drone photography and videography into my repertoire. This allows me to capture stunning aerial views, providing a fresh and unique perspective on events, landscapes, and architectural marvels.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients, from individuals and families to businesses and organizations. Each project is an opportunity for me to collaborate and create something truly special. I approach every assignment with enthusiasm, professionalism, and a commitment to delivering exceptional results. I believe that photography has the power to transcend time and connect people across generations. It is this belief that drives me to continue capturing moments, freezing them in time, and creating visual legacies for my clients.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my work. I look forward to the possibility of collaborating with you and capturing the moments that matter most to you.
